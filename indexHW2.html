Blackjack

Blackjack is the American version of a popular global banking game known as Twenty-One. The main goal is to beat the dealers cards. On table there can be up to seven players. For bigger chance of winning please do study Blackjack strategy.

Players decisions

  • Hit-recieve a card from dealer
  • Stand-stay with initial cards
  • Split-split initial cards that are in same value by increasing bet by 100% and recieving atleast 1 card on each hand
  • Double down-recieving only one card on your initial hand by increasing bet by 100%
